- [Inverting Visual Representations with CNNs by A Dosovitskiy et al. CVPR 2016](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content_cvpr_2016/papers/Dosovitskiy_Inverting_Visual_Representations_CVPR_2016_paper.pdf)-
